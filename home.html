GlowMore Beauty Salon is a leading beauty salon chain in India. It offers a wide range of beauty services and products to its customers. The salon provides services such as hair styling, hair colouring, skin care, make-up, nail art, and body spa. It also offers a range of beauty products such as shampoos, conditioners, styling products, and skin care products. The salon has a team of experienced and trained professionals who provide quality services to its customers. The salon also offers special packages and discounts to its customers. It is a one-stop destination for all your beauty needs.

At GlowMore Beauty Salon, with over 35 years of experience, and a footprint of over 400+ Salons in 125 cities across the length and breadth of the country, we have developed a deep understanding of the beauty industry, its professionals and the skills needed to make a mark. More than 280 entrepreneurs have partnered with Lakmé Lever as franchisees to own and operate Salons across the country. We launched Absolute salon in 2012, which offers bespoke services defining the ultimate facets of hair and beauty. The state-of-the-art salon is currently present in the 3 cities- Delhi, Bengaluru and Mumbai. In 2015, GlowMore Lever formalised a strategic alliance with Aptech – a global leader in vocational training to set up 80 Lakmé beauty academies to train 50,000 stylists across India, over five years. GlowMore Academy powered by Aptech, offers both foundation and advanced level training in skin, hair and make-up.
